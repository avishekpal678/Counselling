About Us

Hi, I'm Avishek Pal, currently studying in my second year at IIEST Shibpur. With a passion for helping students succeed, I have dedicated myself to providing expert counseling support for various entrance examinations.

» Last year alone, I had the privilege of counseling over 100 students for exams such as JEE Main, JEE Advanced, WBJEE, JEXPO, ITI, and nursing entrance tests. Through this experience, I've witnessed firsthand the challenges students face in navigating the complexities of college admissions.

» At Counselling Support Portal, we understand that the college admission process can be overwhelming. With so much information available, it's easy to feel confused and uncertain about the best course of action. That's where we come in.

» Our mission is simple: to provide personalized counseling support that empowers students to make informed decisions and achieve their academic goals. Whether it's updating you on changes in rules, helping you optimize your preference order, or providing timely updates about counseling procedures, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
